Welcome to the unexplored serenity of Assam\u2019s cleanest village. Umswai valley, a land of colourful festivals, untold stories, and pristine natural beauty, is an offbeat rural tourism destination located in the Karbi \u2013Shillong plateau of Assam. This charming valley offers an authentic and enriching experience to the city crowd just 100 km away from Guwahati, the capital city of Assam. Shikhdamkha, Assam\u2019s cleanest village is located in the valley.<\/p>\n

Visit and explore the age-old monoliths, stunning caves, waterfalls, village roads, and enjoy the colourful indigenous festivals of the Tiwa tribe. <\/p>\n
